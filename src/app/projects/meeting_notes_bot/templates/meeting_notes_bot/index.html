Upload transcript from Teams (in .vtt format) How to export transcript from Teams?
To export transcript from Teams:
  1. Open Microsoft Teams and go to Calendar.
  2. Click on the meeting you attended.
  3. Go to Recording & transcript tab.
  4. Click the Download button.
  5. Choose .vtt format.
Note: Only meeting organisers can download transcripts by default.
